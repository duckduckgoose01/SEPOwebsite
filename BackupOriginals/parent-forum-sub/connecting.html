Share Your Story

  • If you believe your experience with special education placement would help families coping with similar issues, please consider sharing your story.

    You may enter your text in the form below, or you may upload a document. Please choose your preference.

    We will not publish your words without first contacting you via email.

  • Accepted file types: doc, pdf, txt, docx, Max. file size: 5 MB.
  • We have some writing prompts to help you get started, if you wish - please select "yes" to see our prompts, or "no" if you would prefer to use your own format.
  • Ideas for things to include: - first name - occupation - family background
  • Ideas for things to include: - first name - age - interests
  • Ideas for things to include: - age of onset - specialists consulted - projected outcomes
